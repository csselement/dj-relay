# Discus market research source notes

- Audience: product stakeholders.
- Delivery mode: portable HTML because no Data Analytics MCP artifact renderer or full Sites publication lifecycle was available in this runtime.
- Report structure mapping: title, Executive Summary, findings with table and bar evidence, recommended next steps, further questions, caveats.
- Chart contract: compare verified public entry prices across substitutes; horizontal/ordinal bar; USD per month or annual monthly equivalent; single measure and no redundant series. The chart demonstrates pricing pressure, not feature-adjusted value.
- Chart omission note: Mixcloud is excluded from the price chart because its current public feature page did not expose a reliable USD checkout price.
- Quantitative caveat: competitor prices differ in billing commitment, limits, audience scale, and included functionality. Exact lookup remains in the comparison table.
- Recommendation basis: desk research plus product inspection. Proposed Discus prices and pilot thresholds are experiments, not observed market benchmarks.
- Legal caveat: the report flags licensing as a launch gate but does not provide legal advice.
